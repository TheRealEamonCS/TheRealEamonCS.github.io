WELCOME TO THE WONDERFUL WORLD OF EAMON CS

The Eamon roleplaying game, initially created by Donald Brown, debuted many years ago on the Apple II. It still enjoys a following to this day due to its nostalgic appeal and the rise of retrogaming.

The system itself is unique - text-based adventuring with tabletop RPG elements and a modular structure that lends itself to user-built scenarios.
